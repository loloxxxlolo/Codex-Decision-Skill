# Example: Travel Decision

## User Question

Should I start a long trip in September, or keep working for a few more months?

## Existing Context

- The user already decided May to August is a paid buffer period.
- Long travel is more suitable after late August.
- Travel should not be used as a way to escape career or life problems.

## Current Answer

Continue preparing for the September travel window, but do not lock the full route too early.

Decision criteria:

Continue long travel:
- cash buffer is enough,
- resignation preparation is stable,
- life state is not collapsing.

Downgrade to short trips:
- cash or energy is only average,
- work handover is not ready,
- long travel would increase anxiety.

Pause travel:
- budget is not enough,
- life rhythm is unstable,
- travel is being used to avoid real decisions.

## Memory Update

Write the confirmed decision criteria into the travel project memory.
